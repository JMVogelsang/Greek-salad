How to prepare a wonderful Greek salad.

by J. Vogelsang

May, 4th 2021

The ingredients:

For the salad

  • \(1/4\) Cucumber
  • \(2\) Tomatoes
  • \(1\) Sweep paprika
  • \(1/4\) Onion
  • \(1/2\) Feta cheese
  • Some olives

For the sauce

  • Olive oil
  • Balsamic vinegar
  • Herbes de Provence
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Optional: sunflower or pumpkin seeds

The preparation

Cut the ingredients for the salad in small pieces. Than add the ingredients for the sauce and mix everything well. Now give the salad some time to marinate. Serve after \(15\) minutes.

Even Plato knew:

Greek salad is the best!
